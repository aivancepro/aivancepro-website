Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

The app won’t launch or displays a blank screen. What should I do?

  1. Make sure you have installed the latest version available on the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Check your Internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  3. Try force-closing and restarting the app.

How can I request the deletion of my data or my account?

You can request the deletion of your account directly in the app settings or by contacting us at aivancepro@gmail.com. We comply with all data privacy regulations.
