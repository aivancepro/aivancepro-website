Personal training is one-on-one guidance from a fitness professional. A personal coach designs programs tailored to your goals, corrects your technique and motivates you during every session.

Traditionally reserved for those who could afford rates of $50 to $100 per session, personal training is now evolving rapidly thanks to technology.

## Benefits of a personal coach

- **Personalized programs** -- adapted to your level, goals and constraints
- **Technique correction** -- injury prevention and movement optimization
- **Constant motivation** -- accountability and support during tough moments
- **Accelerated progress** -- real-time adjustments based on your performance
- **Time savings** -- no more wondering what to do at the gym
